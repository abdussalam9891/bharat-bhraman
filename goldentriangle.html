The Goldentriangle Route

⏳ 10 Days
📍 Jaipur • Jodhpur • Jaisalmer • Udaipur
🏨 4★ Heritage Stays
🚗 Private Car
👥 2–6 People

The Journey

1
Jaipur Arrival

Arrive, check-in, evening walk in local bazaars. Slow introduction to Rajasthan. Stay: 4★ Hotel

2
Jaipur Sightseeing — Forts & Palaces

Visit Amber Fort (elephant ride optional), City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar Observatory, and the Albert Hall Museum. Overnight in Jaipur.

3
Jaipur → Jodhpur (The Blue City)

Drive to Jodhpur (approx. 5 hrs). En route, stop at Ajmer Sharif Dargah and Pushkar Lake. Check in and explore the blue lanes of the old city.

4
Jodhpur — Mehrangarh Fort & Markets

Morning visit to Mehrangarh Fort (one of India's largest), Jaswant Thada memorial, Umaid Bhawan Palace. Afternoon explore Sardar Market. Overnight in Jodhpur.

5
Jodhpur → Jaisalmer (The Golden City)

Drive to Jaisalmer (approx. 5 hrs) through the Thar Desert. Check in and explore Jaisalmer Fort at sunset — the only living fort in India.

6
Jaisalmer — Desert & Dunes

Morning: Patwon Ki Haveli and Bada Bagh cenotaphs. Evening: camel safari at Sam Sand Dunes + desert camp with folk music and dinner under the stars.

7
Jaisalmer → Udaipur (The City of Lakes)

Long scenic drive to Udaipur (approx. 7 hrs). Arrive, check in, and enjoy a sunset boat ride on Lake Pichola with views of the Lake Palace.

8
Udaipur — Palaces & Lakes

Visit City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon Ki Bari, and Fateh Sagar Lake. Evening stroll through the old city and dinner at a rooftop restaurant.

9
Departure

Morning at leisure. Drop to Udaipur airport or railway station for onward journey. Tour ends with unforgettable memories.
