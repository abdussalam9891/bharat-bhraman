The Manali Route

⏳ 5 Days
📍 Manali
🏨 4★ Heritage Stays
🚗 Private Car
👥 2–6 People

The Journey

1
Arrival in Manali — Settle Into the Mountains

Arrive in Manali via Volvo or private car. Check into your hotel and take it easy. Explore Mall Road, nearby cafés, and get your first feel of the mountains.

2
Manali — Temples, Culture & Old Town

Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, and Vashisht hot springs. Spend time in Old Manali exploring riverside cafés and local markets.

3
Solang Valley — Snow & Adventure

Head to Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding, zipline, or snow experiences (seasonal). Optional visit to Atal Tunnel or nearby snow points depending on weather.

4
Kullu & Kasol — Valley Escape

Drive towards Kullu Valley. Visit river rafting points (optional), shawl factories, and continue to Kasol. Walk along the Parvati River and enjoy the laid-back vibe before returning.

5
Departure — Back With a Reset Mind

After breakfast, check out and begin your return journey. You leave Manali refreshed, carrying the calm with you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking — clear, simple, transparent.

Is internet and network available in Manali? +

Network is available in Manali town, but may be limited in remote areas like Solang or Rohtang. It’s best to not rely heavily on internet connectivity during the trip.

Is it safe to travel to Manali in winter? +

Yes, Manali is safe in winter. However, road conditions may vary due to snowfall. Our drivers are experienced with mountain routes to ensure safe travel.

When can I experience snowfall in Manali? +

Snowfall usually occurs from December to February. For snow experiences, winter months are ideal.

Is Rohtang Pass included in the package? +

Rohtang Pass requires special permits and is subject to weather conditions. It is usually not included by default, but we assist with permits and planning.

When is the best time to visit Manali? +

March to June offers pleasant weather and greenery, while December to February is ideal for snowfall and winter experiences.
