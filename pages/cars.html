Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking — clear, simple, transparent.

Is the price shown final or are there extra charges? +

The listed price includes driver and fuel for up to 250 km per day. Tolls, parking, and state taxes (if applicable) are extra. We keep everything transparent — no hidden surprises.

What happens if I exceed the daily 250 km limit? +

No problem — additional distance is charged per km based on the vehicle type. Your driver will always inform you before any extra charges apply.

Are your drivers experienced and verified? +

Yes. All drivers are licensed, background-checked, and experienced in long-distance travel. They are trained to ensure safety, punctuality, and a smooth ride.

Are drivers verified? +

Yes. All drivers are background-checked, licensed, and rated above 4.8.

Do you offer one-way trips or only round trips? +

Both options are available. One-way trips may include a drop charge depending on distance and route.
