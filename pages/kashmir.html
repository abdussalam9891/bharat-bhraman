The Kashmir Route

Discover the Beauty of Kashmir

Experience the magic of Kashmir through a thoughtfully curated journey that blends breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and moments of pure serenity.

Begin in Srinagar, where the iconic Dal Lake, Mughal gardens, and charming houseboats introduce you to the timeless beauty of the valley. Enjoy peaceful Shikara rides and soak in the calm surroundings.

Continue to Gulmarg, a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. From sweeping meadows to the famous Gondola ride, every moment here offers stunning panoramic views.

Explore Pahalgam, where lush valleys, flowing rivers, and scenic trails create the perfect setting for relaxation and exploration. Take in the untouched beauty and slow down amidst nature.

Your journey concludes back in Srinagar, leaving you with unforgettable memories of mountains, lakes, and the peaceful rhythm of Kashmir.

A breathtaking escape for nature lovers, peace seekers, and mountain explorers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking — clear, simple, transparent.

Is the price shown final or are there extra charges? +

The listed price includes driver and fuel for up to 250 km per day. Tolls, parking, and state taxes (if applicable) are extra. We keep everything transparent — no hidden surprises.

Is Kashmir safe for tourists? +

Yes, Kashmir is safe for tourists in major destinations like Srinagar, Gulmarg, and Pahalgam. We also provide trusted local drivers and support throughout your trip.

When can I experience snowfall in Kashmir? +

Snowfall usually occurs from December to February, especially in Gulmarg. For snow experiences, winter months are ideal.

Is the Gondola ride included in the package? +

Gondola tickets are not included as they are subject to availability and weather. We assist you in booking them in advance.

Can I customize my Kashmir itinerary? +

Yes, the trip is fully customizable. You can add destinations, upgrade hotels, or extend your stay based on your preferences.
