Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking — clear, simple, transparent.

Is fuel included in the daily rate? +

Yes. All our rates include driver and fuel for up to 250 km per day. Extra kilometres are charged at the listed per-km rate.

Can I book a one-way trip? +

Yes. One-way bookings are available. A drop charge may apply depending on distance.

What about cancellations? +

Free cancellation up to 48 hours. Within 24 hours, one day charge applies.

Are drivers verified? +

Yes. All drivers are background-checked, licensed, and rated above 4.8.

Can I book for multiple days? +

Yes. Multi-day bookings are our most popular and come with better pricing.
